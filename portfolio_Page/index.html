Knowledge Trove

Knowledge Trove Android Application aim is to help Students to save money from buying new books and helps student to sell their used books. Application provides an easy access to reference books which not easily available online in pdf format which could be downloaded.. Our Application Aims to Address this multifaceted problem by a decentralized platform ,for all students ,to buy,sell or trade textbooks with one another . Our Application can also be used to share handwritten notes in PDF format .It was developed with Google Firebase in backend .
